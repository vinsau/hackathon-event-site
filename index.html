About the Event

What is HackNova?

HackNova is a 48-hour hackathon bringing together the brightest minds in tech. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting your coding journey, this is your chance to build something extraordinary.

Join us at Cebu Technological University, Philippines from December 25-27, 2025 for an unforgettable experience filled with innovation, collaboration, and endless possibilities.
